Business Plan

Concept: Mile Feuille offers artisanal baked goods with a focus on premium taste and presentation, catering to both casual snackers and gourmet dessert lovers.

Target Market: Urban professionals, students, and families seeking high-quality pastries at affordable prices.

Unique Selling Point: Handcrafted recipes using premium ingredients, paired with a modern, visually appealing brand experience.

Pricing Strategy: Competitive pricing with a ₱15 entry price point to encourage repeat customers while maintaining high margins through efficient production.

Marketing Approach: Social media campaigns, influencer partnerships, and pop-up tasting booths to build brand recognition and customer loyalty.

Growth Plan: Expand from direct sales to corporate catering, online delivery platforms, and franchise opportunities within 3 years.
